What Data is Shared?

When using a remote LLM, the following information from your emails will be sent to the service provider:

  • Email subject lines
  • Email body content (cleaned and truncated)
  • Sender information
  • Attachment filenames (not the actual files)
  • Email dates

What is NOT Shared?

  • Your OAuth credentials or access tokens
  • Attachment file contents
  • Your email password
  • Any data stored locally on your machine

Privacy Considerations

  • The remote LLM provider may log requests for debugging and improvement purposes
  • Data transmission occurs over encrypted HTTPS connections
  • You should review the privacy policy of your chosen LLM provider
  • Sensitive or confidential emails should not be processed if you have concerns

  • You can delete all stored summaries using the "Erase All Data" feature
